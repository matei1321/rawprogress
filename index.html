// NUTRITION GUIDES

Evidence-based nutrition principles. No fads. Just what works.

🌿
WHOLE FOODS FIRST

If it grew in the ground, swam in the ocean, or walked on land — eat it. If it was made in a factory — avoid it.

EAT MORE

Meat · Fish · Eggs · Vegetables · Fruit · Nuts · Seeds · Legumes · Root vegetables · Olive oil

AVOID

Packaged snacks · Soft drinks · Fast food · Artificial sweeteners · Processed meats · Seed oils

🚫
CUT THE PROCESSED

If it has more than 5 ingredients or ingredients you cannot pronounce — put it back.

THE 3 BIGGEST CULPRITS

1.Sugar — hidden in almost everything. Read every label.
2.Seed oils — canola, soybean, sunflower. Use olive oil or butter.
3.Refined carbs — white bread, pasta, pastries. Replace with whole grain or root veg.
🦴
PRIMAL EATING

Eat the way humans ate for 99% of history. High protein, natural fats, seasonal carbs. Your body knows how to handle it.

PRIMAL STAPLES

Beef · Lamb · Wild fish · Organs · Eggs · Raw dairy · Berries · Root vegetables · Honey · Bone broth

🥩
PROTEIN IS NON-NEGOTIABLE

Aim for 1.6–2.2g per kg of bodyweight daily. Most people eat half of what they need.

BEST SOURCES

Chicken breast · Beef · Salmon · Tuna · Eggs · Greek yogurt · Cottage cheese · Lentils · Whey protein

💧
HYDRATION

Dehydration kills performance before you even notice it. Drink consistently throughout the day, not all at once.

DAILY TARGETS

Sedentary:2–2.5L per day
Active:3–4L per day
Hot climate:Add 0.5–1L extra
⏱️
INTERMITTENT FASTING

Not a diet — a timing protocol. Compress your eating window and let your body use stored fat for fuel.

POPULAR PROTOCOLS

16:8Fast 16h, eat in 8h window. Most popular.
18:6More aggressive. Good for fat loss phases.
OMADOne meal a day. Advanced. Not for beginners.
